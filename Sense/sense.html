Simple installation.

Sense is a small but powerful computer installed in your electrical panel by a licensed electrician, usually in less than 30 minutes. Using two clamp-on sensors and a 240V breaker, the Sense monitor samples current and voltage one million times per second to determine where your energy is going. Learn more
